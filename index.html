What is Physics

Physics was formely called natural philosophy, it is concerned with those aspect of natural which can be understood in a fundamental wayin term of elementary principles and laws. In the course of time, various specialized science broke away from Physics to form autonomous fields of investigation. in this process, Physics retained its original aim of understanding the structure of the natural world and explaining natural phenomena

The most basic parts of Physics are mechanics and field theory. Mechanics is concerned with the motion of particles or bodies under the action of given forces. The physics of fields is concerned with the orgin nature and properties of gravitation, electromagnetic, nuclear and other force fields. taken together, mechanics and field thoery constitute the most fundamental approach to an understanding o natural phenomena which science offer

In other words, Physics can be defined or summarise as a physical science that deals with the behaviour of matter in relation to energy. Also it can be said to be science measurement.
